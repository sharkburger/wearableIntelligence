# Wearable Intelligence as Perceptual Media

**Author:** Biny Xu · **Type:** Systematic *integrative* review → conceptual framework building · **Started:** 2026-05-31

> Wearable intelligence should be understood not merely as body-worn sensing or AI-enabled computation, but as **perceptual media**: body-worn or body-coupled systems that mediate how users perceive their bodies, environments, and possibilities for action.
